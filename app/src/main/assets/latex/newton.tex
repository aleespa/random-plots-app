\text{Newton's fractal is a boundary set in the}\\
\text{complex plane which is characterized by}\\
\text{Newton's method applied to a fixed}\\
\text{polynomial \(p(z)\). The iteration is given by:}\\
\[ z_{n+1} = z_n - \frac{p(z_n)}{p'(z_n)} \]\\
\text{The points are colored according to which}\\
\text{root of \(p(z)\) the sequence converges to.}\\
\text{Common polynomials include:}\\
\[ p(z) = z^3 - 1 \]\\
\[ p(z) = z^5 - 1 \] \\
\[ p(z) = \sin(z) - 1 \]\\
\[ p(z) = z^6 + z^3 - 1 \]\\
\text{The boundaries between the basins of}\\
\text{attraction of different roots exhibit}\\
\text{extremely intricate fractal structures.}
