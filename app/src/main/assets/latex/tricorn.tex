\text{The Tricorn fractal, also known as the}\\
\text{Mandelbar set, is a fractal set defined by the}\\
\text{iteration of the following mapping:}\\
\[ z_{n+1} = \overline{z_n}^2 + c \]\\
\text{where \(\overline{z_n}\) denotes the complex conjugate}\\
\text{of \(z_n\). Similar to the Mandelbrot set, it is}\\
\text{the locus of points \(c\) for which the sequence}\\
\text{starting at \(z_0 = 0\) remains bounded. Its}\\
\text{characteristic three-cornered shape gives it}\\
\text{its name, emerging from the symmetry of the}\\
\text{antiholomorphic iteration.}
